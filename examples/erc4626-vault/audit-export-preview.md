# Audit Export Preview

The demo Export board generates:

- `proofboard-report.md`
- `proofboard-ledger.json`
- `assumption-debt.md`
- `protocol-map.json`
- `approved-properties.json`
- `generated-foundry-invariants.json`
- `audit-prep.md`

The packet separates reviewed claims, candidate properties, verification evidence, unresolved assumptions, out-of-scope areas, and suggested audit focus.
